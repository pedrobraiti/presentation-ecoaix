Money tips worth sixty seconds of your day.

Finance is the in-house publishing tool behind our educational personal finance channel on TikTok. It turns a single topic — saving, budgeting, debt, investing basics — into a short vertical video with narration, captions and charts, and posts it to our own account.

What the tool does

  • Creates short-form videos. Each video explains one personal finance concept in about a minute, in plain language.
  • Posts to accounts we own. Videos are uploaded to our own TikTok channel through TikTok's official Content Posting API.
  • Used only by our team. There is no public sign-up, no third-party users, and no data collected from viewers.
