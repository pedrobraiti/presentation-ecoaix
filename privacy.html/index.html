Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026

1. Overview

"Finance" (the "Service") is a private, in-house publishing tool that creates short educational personal finance videos and posts them to TikTok accounts owned and operated by us. This policy explains what data the Service touches and what we do with it. The short version: the Service handles credentials for our own accounts only and collects no personal data from viewers or website visitors.

2. Data we collect

  • TikTok account credentials (our own). When our team authorizes the Service with TikTok, we receive OAuth access and refresh tokens, and basic profile information (such as account name) for the TikTok accounts we own. These are used solely to upload and publish our videos through TikTok's official Content Posting API.
  • Nothing from viewers. We do not collect, store or process personal data from people who watch our videos on TikTok.
  • Nothing from website visitors. This website is a static page. It has no accounts, no forms, no analytics, no advertising and sets no cookies.

3. How we use data

OAuth tokens and basic account information are used exclusively to authenticate with TikTok and publish content to our own accounts. We do not use this data for any other purpose.

4. Storage and security

Tokens are stored locally on machines controlled by our team, are not committed to public repositories, and are accessible only to team members. Tokens can be revoked at any time from the TikTok account settings or the TikTok for Developers portal.

5. Sharing and selling

We do not sell, rent, trade or otherwise share any data with third parties. Data is exchanged only with TikTok, through its official API, for the sole purpose of publishing our content.

6. Children's privacy

This website and the Service are not directed at children and do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Your rights

Since we do not collect personal data from viewers or visitors, there is normally nothing for us to access, correct or delete. If you believe we hold personal data about you, contact us and we will address the request in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version, with its effective date, will always be available on this page.
